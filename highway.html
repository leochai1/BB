Highlights


 

Artefact has rendered specialized Project Services for Road Sector for more than 10000 Kms. Services include :-

I. Detailed Design / DPR Preparation
 + 

Artefact has completed detailed design & DPR for more than 681.50 kms of highways & other Roads. DPR prepared by Artefact were held good for construction & were implemented without modifications.

II. Project Management for construction stage
 + 

Artefact, along with Meinhardt Pte, Singapore, has undertaken services as Project Management Consultants for prestigious Chhattisgarh Rural Roads Development project funded by ADB, for Design, Supervision & Construction of 2100 Kms Roads in 6 Districts of Chhattisgarh State with Project Construction cost of Rs.6000 Millions approx.

III. For O & M Design & Supervision
 + 

Artefact has rendered Comprehensive Construction and Operation and Maintenance (O&M) of prestigious Tolled National Highways and State Highways for length exceeding 550 Kms. Salient features of the above O&M Contracts include Service level based contract - Use of Advanced Highways Traffic Management Systems (HTMS), Computerized Automated Toll Systems, various Advanced Techniques in Highway and Public Safety, Public Relations and Law Enforcement, Road Property and Facility Management.
